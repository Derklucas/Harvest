### Biography
Boyd Patterson, a 24-year-old Geology student at Miskatonic University, is equal parts brilliant and absent-minded. Fascinated by the Earth's ancient secrets, he often loses himself in dusty tomes and late-night field studies. Though geology is his official passion, he also harbors a love for astronomy, frequently gazing at the stars and pondering cosmic mysteries. Soft-spoken but enthusiastic, Boyd is always eager to share his latest theories—some of which border on the bizarre.

 Tragically, a year ago, Boyd Patterson’s corpse was discovered at the foot of the Green Mountains; his body mangled by a long fall.

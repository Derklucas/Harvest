### Biography
Daphne Devine was born into a world where a woman’s place was widely regarded as the home. She—quite rightly—resented this unfair and patriarchal view. She still worked hard at Miskatonic University and is seen as one of [[Prof. Roger Harrold |Prof. Harrold]]'s finest students ever.
